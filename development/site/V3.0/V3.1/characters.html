Characters

Profiles, archetypes, and narrative roles of the central figures.

Marlin

Marlin's arc is a portrait of grief-driven control evolving into learned trust. He is driven by fear and love in equal measure.

Nemo

Nemo's wound and curiosity catalyze the story. The small rebellion becomes a crucible for growth for both child and parent.

Dory

Dory embodies a radical, present-moment trust that destabilizes Marlin's control and teaches improvisational wisdom.
